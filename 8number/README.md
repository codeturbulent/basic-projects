# 8 Numbers Game

A logic puzzle where you must fill a grid with numbers 1 to 8 such that no two consecutive numbers are adjacent.

## How to Play
1. Fill each box with a unique number from 1 to 8.
2. Ensure that no two boxes that touch each other (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) contain numbers that are one apart (e.g., 2 and 3 cannot be adjacent).
3. Click "Done" to check your solution.

## Rules
- **Rule 1:** Predecessor and successor can't be besides (horizontally).
- **Rule 2:** Predecessor and successor can't be above or below each other (vertically).
- **Rule 3:** Predecessor and successor can't be diagonal to each other.
