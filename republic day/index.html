    When Where How Why

    .When

    - Republic day is honored on 26th Jan every year as a national festival .

    .Where

    - On 26th Jan Republic Day is celebrated in India .

    .How

    - The Republic Day Parade is the main attraction on this day. People in Delhi attend the parade at Rajpath. Beating the cold weather, Delhites gather in huge numbers and witness the beautiful spectacle .

    .Why

    - Republic Day marks the celebration of the empowerment of Indian citizens to select their own government. It is a national holiday which commemorates the process of establishment of the Indian Constitution.
